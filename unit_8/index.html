About

Miss Soap is a collection of bath bars composed of high-quality, natural ingredients. Harsh chemicals are eliminated in order to nourish and gently cleanse your skin. All products are hand-crafted in Hudson Valley, New York.

Collection

Ingredients

Miss Soap bath bars are based upon a composition of simple yet powerful ingredients. Coconut oil hydrates and moisturizes. Palm oil fights free radicals, preventing skin damage and fine lines. Sunflower oil delivers additional moisture. Additional essential oils and natural ingredients provide the subtle yet distinct frangrance of each bath bar.

Instructions

Warm water is best to preserve the natural oils in your skin. Lather and rinse well. Gently pat dry and moisturize immediately.
